How it works

Review. Refine. Rebuild.

A clear path from not knowing whether your IT setup actually serves you to running an operation that fits your business. Not everyone needs all three — you go as far as your business actually requires, and we tell you honestly when you don't need the next step.

1

Review

A third-party assessment of what your MSP is actually delivering — scope, security, backups, licensing, documentation, and cost. A plain-language report on where the gaps are against your business's real needs.

2

Refine

Clear recommendations on what to keep, right-size, renegotiate, or bring in-house — and whether to stay with your MSP, change providers, or reduce the relationship.

3

Rebuild

Optional hands-on modernization — cloud-first migration, proper tooling, and training your team to run the day-to-day. Built so you own it, not so you depend on us.
